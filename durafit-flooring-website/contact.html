Frequently Asked Questions

How long does installation take?

Installation time varies depending on the project size and complexity. Small rooms typically take 1-2 days, while larger projects may take 3-5 days. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes, we provide completely free consultations and quotes. Our expert team will visit your property, assess your needs, and provide a detailed, no-obligation quote.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve North Shields, South Shields, Newcastle, Sunderland, Gateshead, and surrounding areas in Tyne & Wear. Contact us to confirm coverage for your specific location.

Do you offer warranties?

Yes, all our products come with manufacturer warranties, and we provide our own workmanship guarantee. We'll explain all warranty details during your consultation.
